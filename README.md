## About Me
 
I'm a robotics engineer who loves the intersection of hardware and software. My journey started with Raspberry Pi and Arduino tinkering in school, went deep into low-level & parallel programming during my CS bachelor's, and eventually landed me in a robotics master's — where I got to work on everything from kinematics to keypoint detection.
 
As a student assistant in both teaching and research, I've had the chance to deepen my skills and share them with others.
 
When I'm not coding, I'm either 3D printing something, building a home automation gadget, riding my bike, or out dancing.
